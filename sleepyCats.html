Ahhh, sleep. That wonderful, blissful time when all of the world melts away and dreams come out to play. And what's more snuggly and warm-fuzzy-inducing than a kitty? That's right: a sleeping one.

Welcome to Sleepy Cats, your Internet resource for photos of slumbering Siamese, napping Norwegian Forest Cats, dozing Devon Rexes...and everything in between. When a need for a minute of cuteness calls, give Sleepy Cats a click and enjoy! And if you have shots of your own kitty cuties that you'd like to add to the selection, you're welcome to do that too!
